3D Printing Tomorrow

The future of 3D printing is phenomenal! Imagine someone in need of a kidney being able to get one due to 3D Printing! Jonathan Strickland from FW: Thinking says, “It will redefine the way we think about innovation, design, manufacturing & distribution!” For now, the 3D Printing industry has a good chance of being a booming business until they can start printing working circuit boards, which could wipe out the industry completely! People with a big enough printer could start printing their own 3D printers to their own style, their own customization! Construction crews could create more than five houses in one day, imagine saving those 8-11 months of construction! 8 major career fields are looking into 3D Printing as a huge help to their career field. These 8 career fields are robotics, architecture, fashion, automotive, entertainment, art, food & health.
The 3D Printing manufacturers like MakerBot are looking into using different types of materials to use in 3D printers such as steel, silver, brass & bronze. Even though 3D Printing is making it’s big mark on the world now, it already has an upcoming opponent. 4D Printing is still far away from it’s big debut, but it can be seen in our future. Clearly, 3D Printing is not only great now, but it’s future is quite interesting & magnificent.
