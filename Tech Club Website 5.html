3D Printing Tomorrow

Humans can only imagine what the world will be like if 3D Printing ends up in every household. 3D Printing can revolutionize the world. Imagine how much jobs could be impacted! Sculpters could make 3D models of what they wanted to make, or just paint the 3D model. Architects could make 3D models of their blueprints to make it easy for the construction crew to imagine what the arcitect wanted.
