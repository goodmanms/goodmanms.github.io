Geothermal Energy Overview

 

While sunlight hits the surface of the globe, there is another source of energy, buried underneath the earth. It is geothermal energy. Geothermal energy is defined as energy created by the earth.

Geothermal energy can only occur when water makes contact with molten rock under the earth's crust. These underground sources of power are called reservoirs. All plants must be built on top of these reservoirs. Adding on, These plantsarte in more than 20 countries including the US, Mexico, Italy, Japan, and Israel. Finally, more and more geothermal plants are being developed in different sountries to this day.
