Mars.

People have looked up at the cosmos for generations, studying objects in space. Most twinkle- stars, of course. But there are some that do not twinkle. Particularly, one object has a very red-orange look to it. For years, people have not known what it was. Thanks to many scientists over the years, we now know that this was a planet- Mars.

We have conquered Earth, and stepped foot on the moon, and at the same time, we've been discovering. Discovering places in our universe, exploring their geologic and unique features, and if they could, or even could've harbored life. One planet to be discovered, is right next door. Mars.

We have gotten rovers on Mars. It's about time for humans to step foot on the second planet In it's history.

In fact, today we have gotten farther to getting on Mars than ever before, with groundbreaking technology in development to bring us on the 6 month trip to The Red Planet.
