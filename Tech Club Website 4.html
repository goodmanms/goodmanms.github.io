The 3d printer is divided into different parts. The first part is the Stereolithography. It was recognized as the first part in the 3D printing process. In that part there is the laser and attached to it is the scanner system. The laser beam then goes into the Layers of solidified resin and then into the liquid Resin. The next part is called the DLP aka the digital light system processing similar to the Stereolithography but a little different.  But the DLP does not have a light source. In there is the motor witch gives energy to the stage. Then there’s the Photopolymer and the lense yep you guessed it, reflect light. Up to the photopolymer. Here’s if picture of it. This next part is called the Laser Sintering/Laser melting. Yep you guessed it more lasers. Just can’t get enough of them. Anyway in there is the building Platform witch takes your 3d built object down with a piston to the Ex The same thing but going up for the powders piston. On the Powder is the Roller. Above the roller is the Laser and across it is the scanning mirror witch reflects down to the building Platform. When the object is complete it is sent down to the Extrusion. This next part is called the Extrusion / FDM / FFF. This may be the easiest section so far.  You have your Material Spool and the filament witch goes down to the heated extrusion head. Then the molten material onto the solidified model witch is on the platform and on to the Inkjet. In the inkjet there’s the binder feeders witch have tubes leading to the inkjet Print head. Then with the build piston going down and then the powder feed piston leading up to the powder supply. Then there’s the leveling roller, which levels it. The final Part is the SDL. It has an Adhesive Nozzle, which has a liquid coming out of it. There’s also the Adhesive, which produces the liquid.  Then there’s the Platform going up and down and above it is the Tungsten Carbide blade and above all that is the heat plate. Then there’s the Paper feed mechanism, which feeds the paper to the printer. Final there’s the Pre-printed paper. That’s all the main parts for the 3D printer.
